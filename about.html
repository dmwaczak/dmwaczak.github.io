About Me

Hi, I’m Dylan Waczak, a Senior at the University of Oklahoma pursuing a double major in Finance and Management Information Systems (MIS). I’m passionate about the intersection of finance and technology and am driven to make a positive impact in the industry. Balancing a full course load of 15 credit hours while working 25–30 hours a week at Chuy’s in Norman has strengthened my ability to manage time effectively and prioritize responsibilities.

Outside of academics and work, I’m a proud member of Alpha Tau Omega, one of the nation's largest fraternities, where I actively participate in community service and philanthropy. These experiences have helped me develop strong collaboration, communication, and leadership skills, which I bring to every project and opportunity.

My previous role as a cashier at Home Depot provided valuable experience in customer service, conflict resolution, and technology use, such as point-of-sale systems. This has further fueled my interest in the intersection of finance and technology.

I’m excited about the opportunities ahead and am committed to utilizing my skills and experiences to make a meaningful impact in the finance and technology industry.
